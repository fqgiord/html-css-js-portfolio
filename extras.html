About Me

With Some Experience In The Construction Industry


I excelled in several important roles. As an Excavator Operator , I worked with demolition and earthmoving, ensuring efficiency and safety.


In my role as a Site Engineer, I supervised on-site projects, coordinated teams, and ensured strict compliance with safety, quality, and deadline standards. Additionally, I managed the dismantling and safe removal of industrial chemical process installations, including heat exchangers, pumps, compressors, towers, and piping systems.


Additionally, as a CAD Designer (P&ID), I created and interpreted detailed piping and instrumentation diagrams, ensuring clear communication and precise project execution.


This blend of technical skills and hands-on experience makes me a well-rounded professional, fully prepared to tackle the challenges of modern construction with innovation and excellence.
